OUR STORY

STRAWBERRIES

Grown the right way.

"In the quiet hills of Kodaikanal, The Berry Patch began with a simple idea — to grow strawberries the right way."

We work with local farmers — no shortcuts, no chemicals. Grown organically in Kodaikanal's cool hills, just as nature intended. We chose the Camarosa variety — handpicked and lab-tested by ICAR-IIHR Bangalore with zero pesticide residue across 130+ compounds.
